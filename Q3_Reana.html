About Gojo

Gojo Satoru is a fictional character in Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a jujutsu sorcerer, and a member of the Gojo clan. Loved by fans, he is widely recognized as the strongest sorcerer in the modern era. In Tokyo Jujutsu high, he works as a teacher to train young allies. He is a rather complex individual. With his students, close colleagues, and friends, he often acts playful and laid-back. On the other hand, he is cruel and unsympathetic to sorcerer executives and enemies.

Appearance

Gojo Satoru has a tall frame (6'3), pale skin, and snow-white hair. His blue eyes are overwhelming to stare at, so he wears a black blindfold. For a casual look, he uses sunglasses. His attire includes a dark zip-up jacket with a high collar and black dress boots.

Gojo Satoru's Abilities

Limitless

Satoru's abilities are incredibly powerful. His Limitless Cursed Technique allows him to manipulate space. His Six Eyes grant extreme perception, while Infinity slows anything approaching him.
